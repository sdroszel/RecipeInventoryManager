Recipe Name:

	baked rigatoni

Ingredients:

	- rigatoni
	- pesto
	- crushed tomatoes
	- mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

	1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
	2. Bring a pot of water to a boil
	3. Cook pasta
	4. Drain pasta
	5. Add pasta, pesto, and crushed tomatoes to bowl
	6. Stir to combine
	7. Layer pasta mix and cheese in 13x9 pan
	8. Bake 20 minutes
	9. Serve and enjoy!
