Recipe Name:

	grilled cheese

Ingredients:

	- 2 slices bread
	- cheese
	- butter

Instructions:

	1. Preheat pan
	2. Butter the bread
	3. Add slice of bread butter side down in pan, add cheese, top with slice of bread
	4. cook until golden brown
	5. Flip sandwich
	6. Cook other side until golden brown
	7. remove from pan
	8. Cut and enjoy!
