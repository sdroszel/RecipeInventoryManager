Recipe Name:

	mac and cheese

Ingredients:

	- Box of mac and cheese
	- 6 cups water
	- 4 tbsp butter
	- 0.5 cup milk

Instructions:

	1. Bring water to a boil
	2. Stir in macaroni
	3. Cook 8 minutes, stirring occasionally
	4. Drain noodles
	5. Return noodles to pot
	6. Add milk, butter, and cheese packet
	7. Mix well
	8. Serve and enjoy!
