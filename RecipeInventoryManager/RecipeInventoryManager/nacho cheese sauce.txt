Recipe Name:

	Nacho cheese sauce

Ingredients:

	- 8 oz cheddar cheese
	- 4 oz pepper jack cheese
	- 1.5 tbsp cornstarch
	- 12 oz evaporated milk
	- 2 tsp hot sauce

Instructions:

	1. Over low heat, combine cheese and cornstarch
	2. Add evaporated milk and hot sauce
	3. Whisk for 5 minutes
	4. Serve immediately
